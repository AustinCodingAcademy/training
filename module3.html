Module 3: Phone Interview

Directions: Now that you've made it this far, let's get to know each other. Please schedule a phone interview below. Then follow the directions to prepare for the phone interview.

To prepare for your phone interview:

  • Review content from Modules 1, 2, and 3.
  • Reflect on how you will use what you learned in your classroom.
  • Consider the qualities of a good teacher. Reflect on a teacher that positively impacted your life.
  • Observe yourself and your motivation. Why do you want to teach?
