Module 5: In Person Onboarding

Directions: Follow the steps below to prepare and deliver your first lesson.

Your turn!

We have covered a lot of material, and now it is your turn to show off what you've learned. Prepare a lesson plan for day 2 of your class. You will deliver the lesson at the in person onboarding meeting.

Please bring the following to the meeting:

  • Completed lesson plans for Day 2 of your class
  • Be ready to deliver the lesson and record attendance, grades, and screencasts
  • Voided check
  • Driver's license
  • Social Security Card, Birth Certificate, or Passport
