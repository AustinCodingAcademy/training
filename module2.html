Module 2: Jedi Instructor Rubric

Review the Jedi Instructor Rubric.

Planning

Knowledge of Students

A Jedi knows. By knowing the student, a Jedi makes learning immenent. The deck is set. A Jedi knows.

Lesson Cycle

A Jedi empowers students to realize their potential through intentional processes.

Contribution to Curriculum

A Jedi shares wisdom in every moment.

Teaching

Student Engagement

Learning is inherently joyful. A Jedi teaches students to love learning. Through the love of learning, all accomplishments become possible.

Adult Learning Strategies

A Jedi realizes the needs of the students and uses their natural tendencies to show them their power.

Class Structure

A Jedi structures the teachings, so students will always learn. The deck is set. The student always wins. To learn is to win.

Professionalism

Commitment to Mission

Our task is great, but our resolve is greater! Jedi Instructors do whatever is necessary to push their students to success.

Continued Learning

A Jedi never stops learning. What are you doing to push yourself? Are you a good example for students? To learn is to be.

Ethics and Sensitivity

A Jedi does the right thing, no matter who sees.

Operations

A Jedi operates on the highest level. There is no room for error. Our mission is too great.
